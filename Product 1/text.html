89 JERMYN STREET, London

Floris founder Juan Famenias Floris and his wife Elizabeth began selling perfume, combs and shaving products in the elegant quarter of London’s St James in 1730. The Floris shop they opened at 89 Jermyn Street remains the heart of the business and is still run by their descendants today.
